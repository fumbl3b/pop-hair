ABOUT

I am a seasoned stylist who started my career in Chicago in the '90s. I have worked in many different areas of the city and have finally settled in the vibrant neighborhood of Andersonville. As an independent stylist, I have worked hard to build my brand. I was fortunate enough to meet some amazing clients and friends who have supported me throughout my hair journey.

I specialize in straight edge razor cuts and am also a color specialist for the Kevin Murphy color line. I once lived on the West Coast and still love the "sun-kissed" California blonde. I'm all about easy, lived-in hair that is simple yet full of personality.

- Ken Moore
